## What it does

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Scaffolds projects" icon="rocket">
    `pluggy init` creates a new plugin in seconds. Pick a platform — Paper, Folia, Spigot, Velocity — and go.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Resolves dependencies" icon="puzzle">
    Pulls from Modrinth and Maven. No more hand-editing `pom.xml` or `build.gradle.kts`.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Live dev server" icon="laptop">
    `pluggy dev` runs your plugin against a local server with hot reload on save.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Single binary" icon="download">
    No JDK, no Gradle, no Maven. One executable on your PATH.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
